Get in touch

A calm daily color puzzle, made by one person.

Ordo is built and looked after by an independent solo developer. So that means when you write in, you’re reaching the actual person who built the game. Feedback, bugs, kind words, and hard questions are all welcome.

The best way to reach me
feedback@ordopuzzles.com

I read every message. Since it’s just me, replies can take a few days.

What to write about

  • Feedback & ideas Thoughts on the puzzles, a feature you’d love, or just what’s working. This will genuinely shape where Ordo goes next.
  • Bug reports Something not working right? Let me know your device, your iOS version, and what happened. The more detail, the faster the fix.
  • Press & features Writing about Ordo or looking for assets? Say hello and I’ll get you what you need.

Elsewhere

I also sporatically write and talk about my projects and the learnings I've had from them. If that’s your kind of thing, you can find it over at Tinkerer's Notebook.
